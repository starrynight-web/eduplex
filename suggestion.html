Introduction to Software Engineering

Final Exam Suggestions

  • Theory -
    1. Use Case Diagram - Identify actors & use cases, show relations.
    2. Use Case Description - Describe one use case (name, actors, pre/post, steps).
    3. Risk Analysis - Classify risks, identify from scenario.
    4. Software Testing - Types (Unit, Integration, System, Acceptance), Black-box vs White-box, Functional vs Non-functional.
    5. Project management
  • Mathematics -
    1. Scheduling & Management - CPM, paths, ES/EF, LS/LF.
    2. Software Testing - Cyclomatic Complexity(math).
    3. Cost Estimation - COCOMO, LoC, effort, EV.

Bangladesh Studies

Final Exam Suggestions

  • Economy –
    1. The nature of Bangladesh economy?
    2. Recent trends of Bangladesh economy?
    3. Do you think that the future of Bangladesh economy is bright?/ economic forecast?
  • Six-Point Movement of Liberation war -
    1. What is six points demand/ movement?
    2. Explain the outcome of six points program?
    3. What was six point program and what were they?
  • Climate Change –
    1. What is climate action?
    2. What type of challenges Bangladesh is facing in order to take climate action?
    3. Explain SDG-13?
  • Urban & Rural Development –
    1. Describe push- pull model of lee?
    2. Explain the rural urban migration in Bangladesh (reasons+ impacts)?
    3. Explain SDG-13?
  • ICT Sector In Bangladesh –
    1. What are the challenges of gender equaity in IT sector?
    2. Explain the objectives and key points of IT policy.
