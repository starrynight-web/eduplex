Mid Exam Topics

Grammar, Flowchart, True/False, Paragraphs
  • Grammar - Contains mainly tense, articles and right form of verbs.
  • Flowchart - A diagram that visually represents the steps of a process using symbols and arrows.
  • True/False/Not Given - Identify if it's true, false or not given.
  • Paragraph - Write Descriptive or Narrative paragraphs.

Final Exam Topics

Questions, Sentences, Letters & Essays
  • Multiple Choice – Questions with several answer options where only one is correct.
  • Short Answer Question – Brief questions requiring concise written responses.
  • Heading Matching – Linking headings to the correct sections or paragraphs of a text.
  • Conditional Sentences – Sentences expressing conditions and their possible outcomes.
  • Run-ons and Fragments – Common sentence errors involving incomplete thoughts or improperly joined clauses.
  • Essay – Contains argumentive and Cause & Effect Essays.
  • Letter Writing – Composing formal or informal letters.
