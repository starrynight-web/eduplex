About EduPlex

Our Journey, Our Mission.

EduPlex is a student-driven initiative built by and for the Software Engineering 45th batch of Daffodil International University. Our mission is to simplify learning through structured resources, previous year questions, and powerful academic tools like CGPA calculators.

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate learners and aspiring software engineers who wanted to create a one-stop digital space for students. From study materials to exam routines and tools for GPA calculation — EduPlex is built to empower every learner.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable, collaborative, and technology-enhanced learning ecosystem where students can focus on mastering their subjects instead of wasting time searching for scattered resources.
